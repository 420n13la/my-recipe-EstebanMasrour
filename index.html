Cheese Omelette Recipe

Introduction

This delicious cheese omelette is perfect for a simple breakfast. Bacon or not this is a wonderful breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 egg
  • 100ml Milk
  • Cheese of your choosing

Steps

  1. Put stove on medium
  2. Crack both eggs
  3. Add Milk
  4. Pour onto pan
  5. Cook for 10 minutes
